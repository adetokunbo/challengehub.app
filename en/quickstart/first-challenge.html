Adding Your First Challenge

Build a library of challenges for your courses

Select Challenge Library in the menu (☰).

To add a new challenge to your library, click Add new challenge.

First, at the top of the page, enter a title for your new challenge.

Then, enter details of your challenge on the left side. The fully-formatted result will appear on the right.

In this example, a numerical answer is going to be required, so the units of the answer are specified under "Solution Form".

Next, specify the required response to your challenge by clicking Add answer at the bottom of the screen.

In this case, students must enter an answer of -1 to mark this challenge as complete.

To allow for rounding errors, we specify a tolerance. In this example, the tolerance is 1%, so any answer between -1.01 and -0.99 will be accepted as correct.

After you have specified the tolerance, click Save.

Now you can publish your challenge by clicking Publish challenge in the bottom-right of your screen.

A confirmation box will appear. Type "publish" to confirm that you wish to publish the challenge.

Congratulations! You have added your first challenge to your library!

Go ahead and add some more challenges to your challenge library. When you're ready, learn how to use them to build a course.
