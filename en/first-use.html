First use of ChallengeHub

A short tutorial

Upon logging in to challenge-hub.com, you will be presented with the home screen.

You can find the menu (☰) in the top left corner.

The most important menu items are Challenge Library and Courses.

The workflow is to
1. Create new challenges in the challenge library, then
2. Use those challenges to build courses.

To learn more, click the first link above.
